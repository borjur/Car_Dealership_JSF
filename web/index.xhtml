Car Dealership Management

This software is designed to help you as a car as a car dealership owner

make the bet use of your time by helping to keep your data organized.

Under each category are some common tasks that you as a car dealership owner

would need to perform. For example, you can register a new client. These clients

are individuals who have come into your location and have shown interest in

eventually purchasing a car.

To sell a car to one of these individuals, click the Sell Car button in the

side menu. From there you will select their name, the salesman who sold the car,

the date, and the purchase amount.

You can also register new sellers, see a list of the car inventory, and see sales
reports and records.
